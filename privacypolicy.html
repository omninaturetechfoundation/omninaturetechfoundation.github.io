Privacy Policy

Omni Nature Tech Foundation, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide when you interact with our website ("Site") and related services.


We may collect the following types of information when you use our Site:

1.1. Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and other relevant information that you provide voluntarily when you contact us, sign up for newsletters, or interact with our Site's features.
1.2. Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our Site, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and referring website. This information helps us to improve our Site's functionality and optimize your experience.
1.3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our Site. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.


We may use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1. To Provide and Improve Services: We use your information to deliver the services you request and to enhance your experience on our Site.
2.2. Communication: We may use your contact information to send you relevant information, updates, newsletters, and promotional materials.
2.3. Analytics: We use collected information to analyze trends, monitor usage patterns, and improve the functionality and content of our Site.
2.4. Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.


Sharing Your Information:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:
3.1. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our Site and provide services to you.
3.2. Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.
3.3. Consent: We may share your information with your consent or as otherwise disclosed at the time of collection.


You have the right to:

4.1. Access and Modify Your Information: You can access, correct, or update your personal information by contacting us using the information provided below.
4.2. Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in our emails.


Security:

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.


Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated policy will be posted on our Site along with the effective date.


By using our Site, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
